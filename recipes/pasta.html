Pasta!

Easy as 1-a, 2-a, 3-a

This is literally the easiest recipe you'll ever read. It barely requires a description...mamma mia!

    Ingredients
  • 1 lb of pasta (any type)
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce (Homemade or canned)
  • Basil (optional [but not really]
    Steps
  1. Add water to pot
  2. Boil water and add pasta. Cook until al dente
  3. While pasta is cooking, add sauce to pan and warm
  4. Drain pasta and add drained pasta to pan with sauce
  5. Serve and garnish with basil
  6. MANGIA
